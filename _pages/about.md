Hi, I’m **Mohammad Shamshad** 🌾, born 🎂 into a humble farming family 👨‍👩‍👧‍👦 in Jamalpura village in the [Malerkotla](https://punjabtourism.punjab.gov.in/en/our-destination/malerkotla/) region of Punjab, India 🇮🇳, where I was raised in a close-knit rural environment deeply connected to agriculture 🌱; from an early age, I developed a strong practical understanding of farming and village life through my family’s active involvement in vegetable seedling production 🌱 and crop cultivation 🚜, experiences that not only shaped my daily life but also played a significant role in influencing my interests 💡, values 📚, and long-term aspirations 🌿➡️🌳🏆.
{: .text-justify}

> My educational journey has been one of perseverance and adaptability 💪📚, beginning at a local madrasa 🕌 where I completed my primary education up to Class V, followed by my studies at Islamia Senior Secondary School 🏫 up to Class VIII in the Urdu medium 📝; embracing change with determination 🔄✨, I transitioned to the Punjabi medium for Classes IX and X at the same institution, and driven by a strong interest in science 🔬🧪, I pursued my higher secondary education (+1 and +2) in the Medical stream 🩺 at a Government Senior Secondary School, reflecting my resilience, adaptability, and passion for learning 🌱🚀.
{: .text-justify}

> My academic path has always been deeply rooted in my agricultural background 🌾, where hands-on experience in seedling production 🌱 sparked a lasting interest in plant sciences 🔬 and sustainable farming practices 🌍; with a strong foundation built on both practical exposure and formal education 📚, I chose to pursue a career in agricultural sciences 🚜, and throughout my journey, I remain committed to contributing to the advancement of agriculture 🌿 while staying true to my roots and the values instilled in me growing up 💚.
{: .text-justify}

I work as a **Sugarcane Breeder** 🌱 at **[Punjab Agricultural University (PAU), Ludhiana](https://www.pau.edu/), India** 🇮🇳, with joint appointments at the **[Regional Research Station, Kapurthala](https://pauwp.pau.edu/region-specific-research/)** and the **[Department of Plant Breeding and Genetics, PAU](https://pauwp.pau.edu/plant-breeding-genetics-home/)** 🎓; I lead sugarcane breeding research programs focused on developing high-yielding clones 🌾 with enhanced sugar content 🍬, strong resistance to diseases 🦠 and pests 🐛, improved ratooning ability (regrowth after harvest) 🌿, early maturity ⏱️, and adaptability to diverse agro-climatic conditions 🌍, while also emphasizing the broadening of the genetic base through germplasm conservation 🧬 and hybridization, and integrating advanced tools such as molecular markers 🔬, genomic selection 🧪, and biotechnology ⚗️ to accelerate breeding efficiency and genetic gain, ultimately developing varieties suitable for multiple uses including sugar, bioethanol ⛽, and bioenergy ⚡, thereby supporting sustainable agriculture 🌱 and contributing to societal welfare 🤝.
{: .text-justify}
During my tenure as a **Plant Breeder** 🌱 at the **[Regional Research Station, Faridkot](https://pau.edu/outstations/index.php?_act=manageOutstationData&DO=viewHomePage&intLinkID=3)**, PAU, in affiliation with the **[Department of Plant Breeding and Genetics, PAU](https://pauwp.pau.edu/plant-breeding-genetics-home/)** 🎓, I was actively involved in cotton improvement programs 🌿 focused on the agro-ecological conditions of Punjab 🇮🇳; my work centered on developing and evaluating high-yielding cotton genotypes 🌾 with superior fiber quality traits such as staple length, strength, and fineness 🧵, while also contributing to hybridization and selection programs aimed at combining desirable agronomic performance with resistance to key pests like bollworms 🐛 and whitefly, as well as tolerance to cotton leaf curl virus (CLCuV) 🦠; additionally, I participated in germplasm screening, field trials 🚜, and multi-location testing across environments to identify stable and adaptable lines, with further emphasis on early maturity ⏱️ and plant architecture suited to the cotton–wheat cropping system 🌾🌾, along with the application of modern breeding tools 🔬 to enhance selection efficiency and accelerate varietal improvement.
{: .text-justify}
I worked as a **Post-Doctoral Researcher** 🎓 at the [Transforming India's Green Revolution by Research and Empowerment for Sustainable food Supplies (TIGR2ESS)](https://tigr2ess.globalfood.cam.ac.uk/fps/FP3) programme at the [University of Cambridge](https://tigr2ess.globalfood.cam.ac.uk/staff/dr-mohammad-shamshad), UK 🇬🇧, where my research focused on enhancing wheat’s resilience 🌾 to heat 🔥 and drought 💧 stress, which are critical challenges for global wheat production amid climate change 🌍; my work involved identifying and understanding the genetic 🧬, physiological 🧪, and molecular ⚗️ mechanisms that enable wheat to withstand extreme temperature and water scarcity conditions, including mapping genes associated with heat and drought tolerance, using genetic markers for breeding more resilient wheat varieties 🌱, and exploring plant traits such as water use efficiency 💧 and root architecture 🌿 to improve survival under stress, with the ultimate goal of supporting breeding programs that develop climate-resilient wheat varieties 🌾, ensuring long-term agricultural sustainability 🌍 and strengthening global food security 🍞.
{: .text-justify}
My work focuses 🎯 on the development and evaluation of high-yielding, disease-resistant, and abiotic stress-tolerant crops. [shamshad.in](https://www.shamshad.in) is my blog to keep track 🗒️ of my projects and different HOW-TOs related to data 🔬 and tech 📡.
{: .text-justify}
I am passionate about Agriculture and cutting-edge technologies. I have a solid 🖊️ understanding and rigorous 📌 hands-on experience in plant breeding, quantitative genetics, computational biology ⚙️ and high-throughput phenotyping, earned during my academic and research work with plant breeders/geneticists, bioinformaticians, and pathologists across my undergraduate, graduate, and postdoctoral career.
{: .text-justify}
I have professional expertise in Plant Breeding, Quantitative Genetics, Genomics, and Computational Biology. I am passionate about Experimental Design, Data Analysis, and Visualization using R, SAS, JMP SAS, Python, UNIX/Linux, HTML/CSS, Git, and GitHub. I also have hands-on laboratory experience in DNA extraction, PCR, Gel Electrophoresis, and Microscopy 🔬.
{: .text-justify}
